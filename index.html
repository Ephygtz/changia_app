Nancy Chep

Nancy Chep is a form 3 student from Kapsabet county, Kilmanget Secondary School.She had an outstanding fee balance of 12000 which she could not meet by the set deadline. The school submitted her case to the web app and through changiaApp a total of 49,000 was raised. This funding has paid for her school fees for the next 3 terms providing her parents ample time to prepare for the next term payments. :-)

  • Student: Nancy Chep

Asman and Sunra Mohamed

These sisters completed their primary education with flying colors. lack of funds to pay for secondary education almost halted their dreams of becoming full stack developers. A neighbour submitted their case to the changiaApp and now the sisters are in secondary school through the funds raise d plus they were also bought for laptops with development kits

  • Student: Asman and Sunra Mohamed

Maasai Mara Primary School

Maasai Mara Primary School submitted an appeal to well wishers using ChangiaWebApp to source funding for purchase of desks for the students. A total of 120000 was raised over a 3 month period and now the school has 500 new desks.

  • Client: Maasai Mara Primary School.
